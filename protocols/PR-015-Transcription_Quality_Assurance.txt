Standardised quality assurance procedures ensuring transcription accuracy, 
consistency, and professional output quality. Applies to all Afrikaans/English 
bilingual transcriptions regardless of length or format.

================================================================================
PHASE 1: PRE-TRANSCRIPTION ASSESSMENT
================================================================================

1.1 AUDIO QUALITY EVALUATION
----------------------------
Rate on 1-5 scale:

5 - EXCELLENT
- Crystal clear audio
- No background noise
- All speakers clearly distinguishable
- Professional recording quality

4 - GOOD
- Clear audio with minor issues
- Minimal background noise
- Speakers mostly distinguishable
- Occasional volume variation

3 - ACCEPTABLE
- Understandable with effort
- Moderate background noise
- Some speaker overlap
- Requires concentration

2 - CHALLENGING
- Difficult to understand
- Significant background noise
- Frequent speaker confusion
- Multiple inaudible sections expected

1 - POOR
- Very difficult to understand
- Heavy background interference
- Speakers often indistinguishable
- May require audio enhancement

ACTION THRESHOLDS:
- Score 4-5: Proceed normally
- Score 3: Note in transcript header, allow extra time
- Score 2: Inform client, request enhancement if possible
- Score 1: Recommend audio enhancement before transcription

3.4 INAUDIBLE/UNCLEAR REVIEW
----------------------------
For each marked section:
1. Re-listen with volume boost
2. Re-listen with headphones
3. Try different playback speed
4. If still unclear, mark remains
5. Document percentage of inaudible content

QUALITY THRESHOLDS:
- <1% inaudible: Excellent
- 1-3% inaudible: Good
- 3-5% inaudible: Acceptable
- >5% inaudible: Note in delivery, may need client review

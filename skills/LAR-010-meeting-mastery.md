## Effective Meeting Practices

### Before the Meeting
- Send agenda 24+ hours in advance
- Include pre-reading with time estimates
- Confirm attendance
- Prepare any presentations/materials

### During the Meeting
- Start on time
- Assign timekeeper
- Capture actions in real-time
- Park off-topic items
- End on time

### After the Meeting
- Distribute minutes within 24 hours
- Follow up on actions
- Track progress in next meeting
- Escalate blockers
